King Lear - Act I, Scene II (Continued)

Scene: The Royal Palace

KENT

Good my liege, allow me to speak with Cordelia. She is misunderstood.

KING LEAR

Let her speak no more. I am done with her. She has renounced my love.

CORDELIA

I beg your majesty, listen to me. I do love you as I am bound, but my words are few.
